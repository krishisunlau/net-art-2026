TAKING IN THE AREA

You get to a vibrant part of the city where you feel the hustle and bustle of people living their lives. You look around and there is a lot to take in.

One thing that catches your eye is an open air art exhibition titled "Ambitions". That seems relevant to your new move and you are intrigued.

Another thing that catches your attention is a street musician that is garnering a crowd around him. He is playing a beautiful melody that reminds you of your childhood.

Which do you go see?

→ The art exhibition

→ The street musician
