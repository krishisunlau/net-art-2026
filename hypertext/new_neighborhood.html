THE BOOK CAFE

You keep wandering the city, now away from the crowds. You want to get your steps in so you start taking random turns on street corners.

You walk for what feels like forever and end up in a village-like part of the city filled with cottage-core town homes and beautiful greenery. There is a small bookstore cafe on the corner of the next street and you decide to stop to get something to eat.

You enter and there is a massive collection of books... shelves stocked from floor to ceiling. You decide you just HAVE to pick one and start reading.
